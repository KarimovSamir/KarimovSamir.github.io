CalLab - Who we are

CalLab is a modern calibration and testing laboratory, fully accredited to AZS ISO/IEC 17025:2020. We offer calibration, testing, and verification services with high technical expertise. Established in 2020, we are part of Deniz Service LLC and operate under an ISO-certified management system

About us

Deniz Service is an experienced supply and engineering provider, established in 2005. We are fully equipped to provide complete turnkey solutions, covering the entire cycle from the development of project documentation to the final stages of installation and commissioning. Our approach ensures smooth execution, technical excellence and full compliance with customer expectations.

How we act?

We act as a reliable EPC (engineering, procurement and construction) partner, especially in the steel sector, bringing engineering expertise, reliable procurement and practical project execution within a single cycle. Deniz Service has assumed full responsibility as an EPC contractor in many projects in the steel industry.

Our reach spans across three key operational hubs:

  • • Kazakhstan – Represented by Duvall Holding LLP
  • • Azerbaijan – Head Office
  • • United Arab Emirates (for supply to Azerbaijan) – Represented by Elmidel LTD

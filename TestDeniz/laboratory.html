Professional Calibration Services

CalLab is a modern calibration laboratory, fully accredited to AZS ISO/IEC 17025:2020. We offer calibration and verification services with high technical expertise. Established in 2020, we are part of Deniz Service LLC and operate under an ISO-certified management system

Our labs

Our laboratories cover a wide range of areas including pressure, temperature, electrical, mass and weight, dimensional, high voltage, torque, noise, load, gas, and volume.
